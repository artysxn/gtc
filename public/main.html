How to Play

The Setter

  • Choose a secret location on the map.
  • Must be a city/town with >5k population.
  • Upload a photo (or paste with Ctrl+V) that represents the spot.
  • Wait for players to find you!
  • You can give up your role if you're stuck.

The Guessers

  • Examine the photo and search the map.
  • Click or type to guess a location.
  • Distance Rings appear to guide you.
  • Powerups unlock at 30, 60, 90 total guesses.
  • First to guess the exact location (same place) wins!
